## Feature Pillars

### 1. Turn Engagement into Revenue (Monetization)
Bring in more income with built-in monetization tools straight from the bio link.
- **Sell Products and Merch:** Manage inventory, pricing, orders, and fulfillment.
- **Services & Booking:** 1-on-1 sessions, classes, coaching, consulting.
- **Events and Tickets:** Create, sell, RSVP, and manage in-person/online events (one-time or recurring).
- **Sell Digital Files:** Courses, ebooks, templates, and guides.
- **Paywalls to Content:** Require payments or sign-ups to unlock exclusive content.
- **Memberships & Subscriptions:** Recurring payments and packages.
- **Affiliate Links & Codes:** Multiple links and coupon codes management.
- **Dropshipping & Print on Demand:** Sell without managing inventory.

### 2. Own Your Audience
Take control of community relationships beyond social media platforms.
- **Capture Leads:** Forms to capture emails and inquiries.
- **Manage Relationships (CRM):** Track interactions, tag contacts, add notes.
- **Automate Growth:** Welcome emails, cart recovery, and follow-ups that run 24/7.
- **Send Emails:** Built-in professional email marketing with templates and scheduling.

### 3. Link, Share & Grow
Tools built to scale and make every click count.
- **Branded Link Shortener:** Trackable short links for credibility.
- **Advanced Analytics:** Real-time tracking of clicks, sales, and behavior.
- **SEO Optimization:** Get indexed on Google with custom titles and meta descriptions.
- **Landing Pages on Demand:** Instant single pages for promos or launches.
- **Custom Domain:** Use a custom URL or get a free one with premium plans.

# Competitor 2: Lnk.Bio
*Positioning: "One URL & Multiple Links" – Safe, Easy, and Ethics-Driven.*

## Core Value Proposition
- "Share all your Social Media profiles and important links with just one url. It literally takes 1 minute to get started."
- Heavily focused on ease of use ("Log in via Instagram") and deep customization ("3000 Social, Music & Contacts icons").

## Ethical Branding ("Our Cores")
*Unique approach to company branding by highlighting internal values:*
- Black Owned Business, Woman Owned & Led, LGBTQIA+
- 75% Diverse team, Menstrual Leave, Work-Life Balance
- Independent Business, No Data Sold, No Corporate Greed
- "Free Pro for equity Activists"

## Feature Highlights
- **Unlimited Links & Temporary Toggles:** "Got a new sponsored campaign? Put it on top. Choose to temporary disable all other links!"
- **Safe & Official:** Uses official Instagram API, no password required.
- **Web-based:** No app required; works across all devices.
- **Deep Customization:** 28,179 Fonts, Wallpapers, Favicons, Video Wallpapers, Mirrors IG Grid.

## Iterative Pricing Strategy
Lnk.Bio uses an aggressive tiered pricing model with both subscription and one-time options:

### 1. Free ($0)
- Unlimited Links, Music & Video embeds, Maps
- Basic stats, 34 Embeds, 14 Integrations, 3 Pages, 442 Templates

### 2. Mini ($0.99/month)
- Scheduled Links, Link Groups, Password-protected Links
- External Pixels, IG/TikTok Sync, Advanced Analytics, UTM Presets, 5 Pages

### 3. Unique ($24.99 one-time - no subscription)
- **Extremely popular model for creators tired of SaaS.**
- Remove Logo, Custom Colors, Shop, Newsletter, Contact Form, Carousels, 10 Pages.
- Free `.bio` domain for a year.

### 4. Ultra ($89.99/year)
- Platform Verification (Blue Tick)
- Connect Existing Custom Domain
- Booking Calendar, AI Translations

### Add-ons (A la carte)
- Blue Tick ($2/mo), Booking Calendar ($4/mo), Translations ($3/mo), Custom Domain ($40/yr).

## Payment Diversity
*Massive global focus:* Supports 33+ localized payment methods, including PayPal, Alipay, Spei, Pix, GrabPay, GCash, mPesa, and more. Highly localized strategy.
